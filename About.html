About Mirul Moltadir Khan

Mirul is born in Gazipur in the year 1993. After passing his SSC and HSC from Dhaka board, he completed his B.Sc degree from IUBAT. Now he is working as an engineer in Saad Group of Industries. Before joining Saad group, he worked as an executive in Palmal Group under Arkay Knit Dyeing Mills Ltd.
